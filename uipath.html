UiPath Overview

UiPath is a global leader in Robotic Process Automation (RPA). With offices in over 25 countries, UiPath helps organizations automate repetitive digital tasks, enabling faster operations and greater accuracy.

Technologies Used

  • UiPath Studio & Orchestrator
  • C#, .NET, VB.NET
  • Python for scripting
  • API integration
  • Cloud services (Azure, AWS)

Hiring Details

  • Roles: RPA Developer, Automation Engineer, Support Engineer
  • Eligibility: Minimum 60%, strong logical & programming skills
  • Preferred Skills: .NET/Java, problem-solving, RPA certification (optional)
  • Locations: Bangalore, Chennai, Remote options available
  • CTC Offered: ₹5 – ₹9 LPA (may vary with experience)

Hiring Process

  1. Online Coding Assessment
  2. Technical Interview (RPA + Programming)
  3. Managerial/HR Round

Why Join UiPath?

  • Work with cutting-edge RPA technology
  • Collaborative and diverse work culture
  • Flexible working hours & remote opportunities
  • Training, certification sponsorships, and fast growth paths
