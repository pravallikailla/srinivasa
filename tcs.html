TCS Overview

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a global IT services, consulting, and business solutions company, headquartered in Mumbai, India. It is a major part of the Tata Group and has a large global presence with operations in 46 countries and over 150 locations. TCS focuses on helping businesses transform through innovation and technology, offering services across various industries like finance, healthcare, and retail.

Technologies Used

  • Java, Python, C++, .NET
  • Cloud Platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Data Analytics & AI/ML
  • DevOps Tools
  • Enterprise Solutions (SAP, Oracle)

Hiring Details

  • Roles: Software Engineer, System Analyst, QA Engineer
  • Eligibility: 60% throughout academics, no active backlogs
  • Preferred Skills: Programming, Communication, Teamwork
  • Locations: PAN India (Hyderabad, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, etc.)
  • CTC Offered: ₹3.5 – ₹7 LPA

Hiring Process

  1. Online Aptitude + Coding Test (NQT)
  2. Technical Interview
  3. Managerial + HR Interview

Why Join TCS?

  • Global exposure and work culture
  • Strong learning and training programs
  • Stability and brand reputation
  • Opportunities in diverse domains and technologies
